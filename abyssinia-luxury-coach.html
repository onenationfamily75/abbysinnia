Who We Are

East Africa's Premier Cross-Border Coach

Abyssinia Luxury Coach pioneered the first direct luxury ground link between Nairobi, Kenya and Addis Ababa, Ethiopia — a historic journey of over 1,500 kilometres through the iconic Moyale corridor.

Inaugurated in August 2025, our service fills a long-standing gap in cross-border overland travel, offering a comfortable, affordable, and scenic alternative to air travel for traders, tourists, and families across East Africa.

We operate fully equipped Daewoo sleeper coaches with reclining seats, climate control, and attentive crew — making every kilometre a pleasure.

Common Questions

FAQ

You will need a valid passport and the appropriate visa for both Kenya and Ethiopia. East African passport holders may have different requirements. Please check with the relevant embassies before travel. Our crew will assist with border procedures at Moyale.
Each passenger is allowed up to 20 kg of checked luggage and one piece of hand luggage. Additional luggage or oversize items may attract a small fee. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange cargo or parcel shipments.
After completing the booking form, you will be redirected to WhatsApp to confirm your reservation with our team at +254752606760. You can pay via M-Pesa or cash at our offices. Your seat is confirmed once payment is received.
Yes! We offer a parcel and cargo service between Kenya and Ethiopia. Contact us on WhatsApp at +254752606760 for pricing and availability based on size and weight of your shipment.
Cancellations made more than 48 hours before departure receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a 20% fee. No-shows are non-refundable. Contact us on WhatsApp as soon as possible if your plans change.
Meals are not included but we make scheduled stops at quality restaurants along the route. You will have time to purchase food and drinks at your own expense. We recommend bringing snacks for the journey as well.
